What We Do

Six Specialisms.
One Integrated Studio.

From the first sketch to the final handover, VOAD offers complete architectural and interior design services tailored to each client's vision — residential, commercial, or heritage.

01

Residential Architecture

Bespoke homes designed around how people actually live — including bungalows, villas, and farmhouses tailored to the Gujarat climate and your family's lifestyle. Every plan begins with understanding how you want to inhabit space.

02

Interior Design

Refined interiors for residences, kitchens, suites, and premium hospitality environments. We work with materials, light, and proportion to create spaces that feel both sophisticated and deeply personal.

03

Commercial & Office Design

Workspaces designed to support productivity, reflect brand identity, and attract talent. From boutique studios to corporate offices, we design environments that communicate who you are before a word is spoken.

04

Heritage Designs

Sympathetic restoration and adaptive reuse of historic havelis and heritage buildings. We preserve original craftsmanship — carved jharokhas, lime plaster, stone floors — while bringing spaces into contemporary use.

05

Renovation & Remodeling

Transforming existing spaces through improved layouts, upgraded materials, and refined finishes. Whether a single room or an entire floor, we approach renovation with the same rigour as new construction.

06

Turnkey Solutions

Complete project execution from concept development through construction supervision to final handover. One point of contact, full accountability — so you can focus on your life while we build your space.

About VOAD

Design Rooted
in Purpose

At VOAD Architecture & Interiors, we believe great design goes beyond aesthetics. It is about creating environments that enhance how you live, work, and connect. Led by Ar. Vivek Bosmiya with nine years of practice across Gujarat and India, our Rajkot-based studio transforms client visions into cohesive, meticulously detailed spaces that stand the test of time.

We work across residential architecture, interior design, commercial projects, heritage restoration, and turnkey delivery — bringing the same level of rigour and thoughtfulness to every project regardless of scale.
