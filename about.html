About VOAD

Design rooted in purpose, craft, and clarity

Our Philosophy

Elevating Standards
Through Purposeful Design

VOAD is a Rajkot-based studio founded by Ar. Vivek Bosmiya. We design homes, interiors, commercial spaces, and heritage structures, and we take the same care with a single room as with an entire building. Nine years in, we still approach every project like the first one: curious, thorough, and genuinely invested in getting it right for you.

We stay involved from the first sketch all the way to handover. That means you have one person to call, one team accountable, and a space at the end that actually matches what you imagined at the start.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make, from the scale of a material choice to the structure of a project timeline.

■

Listening Before Drawing

Every project begins with understanding your lifestyle, your aspirations, your constraints. We do not begin designing until we are certain we have heard what matters most.

□

Detail as a Discipline

The difference between good and exceptional is almost always in the details. We give the same attention to a door handle profile as to a structural column, because our clients will live with both.

△

Honest About Timelines

We set realistic schedules and keep to them. If something changes, you hear about it from us directly, before it becomes a problem.

○

Material Integrity

We recommend materials for their suitability, durability, and authenticity, not their margin. Local stone, handcrafted joinery, and honest finishes are always preferred over the decorative shortcut.

◇

Rooted in Context

Architecture should belong to its place. We design with the Gujarat climate, craft traditions, and local building culture in mind, creating spaces that feel native to their setting.

◆

One Studio Accountable

You work with Ar. Vivek Bosmiya from start to finish. No hand-offs to junior staff, no diluted attention. Full accountability, one point of contact throughout.
