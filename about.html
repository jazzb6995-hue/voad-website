About VOAD

Design rooted in purpose, craft, and clarity

Our Philosophy

Elevating Standards
Through Purposeful Design

At VOAD, we believe great design goes beyond aesthetics. It's about creating environments that enhance your life, your work, and your connections. Led by Ar. Vivek Bosmiya with nine years of practice, our studio transforms client visions into cohesive, well-detailed spaces — from concept and design development through to construction supervision and turnkey handover.

The Studio

The Person Behind VOAD

01

Ar. Vivek Bosmiya

Principal Architect & Interior Designer

Vivek founded VOAD with a clear conviction: that architecture should serve people, not impress them. Over nine years and more than 70 completed projects, he has built a practice defined by careful listening, considered detail, and an unwillingness to treat any two projects as the same.

His work spans private residences, corporate interiors, hospitality spaces, and the sensitive restoration of Gujarat's historic havelis — each approached with the same rigour and the same commitment to the client's vision. Based in Rajkot, VOAD is available for projects across India.

Six Design Specialisms

Our Reach

Based in Rajkot.
Designing Across India.

VOAD is rooted in Gujarat, with a deep familiarity with local craft traditions, materials, and building culture. We are available for residential, commercial, and heritage projects anywhere in India, and regularly travel to site wherever the work demands it.

Every project we take on receives the same level of personal attention. We deliberately keep the studio small so that Ar. Vivek Bosmiya remains the lead designer on every commission — from the first conversation to the final walk-through.
